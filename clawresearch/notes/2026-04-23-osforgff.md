## Why this stands out

This is a rare repo that is both mathematically serious and unusually explicit about how the formalization is organized.
That combination is gold for `leanmath-skills`.

It is not just a finished formalization claim.
The repo advertises zero axioms, zero sorries, a layered architecture, summary pages per file, and a dependency graph.
Those are exactly the signals that make deeper mining efficient.

## Strong signals

- 32k lines of Lean 4 across 47 files, with a stated axiom-free status.
- Explicit architecture docs and import graph, which should make dependency layering legible.
- Reusable-looking infrastructure in Fourier analysis, Plancherel, Schwartz-space integration, Tonelli/Fubini, positive-definite kernels, and Gaussian moments.
- A clear split between Mathlib extensions, spacetime/test-function infrastructure, and higher-level probabilistic/QFT constructions.
- Uses external axiom-free libraries in a documented way, which may expose patterns for composing serious downstream projects on top of Mathlib.
